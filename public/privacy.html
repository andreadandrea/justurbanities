Privacy notice — Justurbanities (English)

1. Data controller

[ERASMUS+ PROJECT CONSORTIUM / LEAD ORGANISATION — address — contact email]. Data protection contact: [TO BE COMPLETED].

2. What we process

Email and password (hashed) — only if you create an account, to sign in and resume your game across devices. Display name — to identify you in the class session (a made-up name is fine). Cloud save — only when you use "Save to cloud". Game events (choices, progress) — only in classroom mode, to build the shared city and the educational report. Single player works fully offline: with no account and no classroom mode, no data leaves your device.

3. Legal basis and minors

Processing is based on consent (art. 6.1.a GDPR). For students below their country's age of digital consent (13–16), registration requires consent from a holder of parental responsibility, collected through the school according to the project's procedures. [PROCEDURE TO BE DEFINED]

4. Where and for how long

Data is hosted on Supabase (processor) in a data centre in the European Union. Class-session events are automatically deleted after 30 days; the teacher can erase a whole session at any time in one action. Your account and cloud save persist until you ask for deletion.

5. Your rights

You may request access, rectification, erasure, restriction and portability by writing to [CONTROLLER EMAIL]. You have the right to lodge a complaint with your supervisory authority.

6. What we do NOT do

No advertising, no profiling, no sharing with third parties, no tracking beyond what is described above.
